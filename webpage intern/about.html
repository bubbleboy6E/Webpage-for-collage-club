The Department of Computer Science and Engineering (Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning) was established in 2020 with a commitment to excel and deliver results with a clear focus. It has an annual intake of 60 and has subsequently increased to 120, besides an additional 20% under lateral entry scheme. The department has well experienced faculty members who possess quality teaching with a passion towards high quality research. They are also knowledgeable, hard-working, earnest and adoptable, well-informed and dosed with a sense of responsibility. The AIML Tech Club is a vibrant community of tech enthusiasts who are passionate about the field of Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning. This club provides a fun and engaging platform for its members to share knowledge, learn new skills, and collaborate on exciting projects. From creating intelligent chatbots to developing innovative predictive models, the club is always exploring the latest AI and ML technologies. Through workshops, seminars, and hackathons, members can deepen their understanding of these cutting-edge technologies and gain valuable hands-on experience. Joining the AIML Tech Club is an excellent way to stay up-to-date with the latest developments in this dynamic field and connect with like-minded individuals who share your passion for technology.
